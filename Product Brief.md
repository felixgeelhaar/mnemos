# Mnemos Product Brief

# Product Vision

Mnemos turns any input into structured, evolving, and evidence-backed knowledge, serving as the definitive source of truth for complex decision-making.

# Target Users

* AI Engineers: Need reliable ground truth for RAG and model evaluation.  
* Knowledge Workers: Managing high volumes of research and documentation.  
* Product Teams: Tracking decision history and cross-functional alignment.

# Core Problems

* **Loss of Reasoning:** Context behind historical decisions is often lost over time.  
* **Fragmented Knowledge:** Critical data is siloed across Slack, Docs, and emails.  
* **Conflicting Truths:** Different sources provide contradictory information with no resolution.  
* **AI Unreliability:** Standard AI outputs lack verifiable evidence and citations.

# Desired Outcomes

### Primary

Empower users to answer: "What is the current state of truth and what is the evidence supporting it?"

### Secondary

* Understand the evolution of a claim over time.  
* Proactively identify and resolve knowledge contradictions.  
* Build high trust in AI outputs through transparent sourcing.

## Product Bets

- Inputs → knowledge claims improve understanding.
- Surfacing contradictions increases trust.
- Evidence-grounded answers beat ungoverned RAG.

## User Flow

1. Add inputs (notes, transcripts, docs).
2. Mnemos extracts structured claims with evidence.
3. Ask a question.
4. Get a structured answer with citations and surfaced contradictions.

## Success Metrics

- < 5 min time-to-value
- ≥ 70 % structured answers
- Contradictions visible to the user

## Risks

- Poor extraction quality.
- False-positive contradictions.
- Perception that Mnemos is "just RAG".

Definition of Success Users can reliably understand what happened and why.
