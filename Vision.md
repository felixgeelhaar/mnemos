# Mnemos — Vision

## Vision Statement

Mnemos becomes the system of record for what is true over time—for humans and AI.

---

## The Problem

The foundation of modern enterprise and AI systems is broken. Knowledge decays, becoming:

- **Fragmented:** Scattered across siloed tools, documents, and message threads.  
- **Contradictory:** Lack of version control makes it impossible to know which information is current or authoritative.  
- **Ungrounded:** Essential context, evidence, and history are constantly lost.

This structural failure costs organizations millions in repeated work and leads to unreliable, unexplainable AI outputs.

## The Cost of Inertia

Relying on decaying, unverifiable knowledge results in:

- **Decision Debt:** Slow, expensive, and low-confidence decisions for humans.  
- **AI Hallucination:** Systems that invent facts, forget context, and break trust.  
- **Operational Friction:** Massive engineering and knowledge-worker toil.

---

## The Shift

We are moving from:

documents → understanding

to:

inputs → knowledge → truth

Mnemos introduces a new layer:

---

## Strategic Phasing

Mnemos will deploy its layer of truth through a phased, domain-specific approach:

- **Phase 1: Developer Primitive (The Data Infrastructure Layer):** Establish Mnemos as a local-first, open-source knowledge engine, providing the essential APIs for persistent memory and grounded reasoning for AI agents and developer tooling.  
- **Phase 2: Team Knowledge Engine (The Institutional Memory Layer):** Expand to provide auditable, traceable institutional memory for knowledge workers and product teams, directly solving internal contradictions and preserving historical context for long-lived projects.  
- **Phase 3: Cognitive Infrastructure (The System of Truth):** Evolve into a backend standard for complex decision systems and enterprise AI, becoming the critical, versioned system of record for "what is true" across entire organizations.
